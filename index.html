Meditation

Mindul Meditation

Meditating is practicing focusing the mind and developing sense of awareness ,clarity and calmness

How to Meditate.

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable space
  2. It is suggested that you are sitting comfortable
  3. Close your eyes
  4. Take a deep breath in then out
  5. Focus on your breathing
  6. If you feel your mind wander ,its okay
  7. Be aware of your wandering thoughts
  8. Gently refocus your thoughts to your breathing
